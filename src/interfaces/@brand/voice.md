# Voice & Terminology

## Tone

Friendly and warm. Casual but not sloppy. The app feels like a helpful friend, not a corporate product. Sentence case, natural punctuation.

- "What's your name?" not "ENTER YOUR NAME"
- "Thinking..." not "Generating response, please wait"
- "Something went sideways" not "Error 500: Internal Server Error"

## Error Messages

Keep it human. Never expose technical details — no HTTP codes, no stack traces, no "contact support." Just acknowledge what happened and suggest trying again.

## Terminology

| Instead of | Use | Why |
|------------|-----|-----|
| Submit | Say Hello | Specific to what's happening |
| Loading | Thinking... | More human |
| Error | Oops | Gentle |
